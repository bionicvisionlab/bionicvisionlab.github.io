Join Us

In general, we are looking for enthusiastic, computationally minded individuals with a shared passion for bionic vision. If you are interested in joining us, make sure we have open positions (see below) and check out our Lab Manual to familiarize yourself with our lab culture, policies, and expectations.

Please note that we get a lot of emails from prospective PhD students, so Michael will not be able to respond to messages that aren’t clearly tailored to the lab’s vision (“I’m also interested in machine learning” is not specific enough). The next deadline to join us is December 2022.

Open Positions

Looking to get some paid research experience during your pre-PhD gap year?

We are inviting applications for a full-time Junior Specialist with an anticipated start date of July 1, 2022 - September 1, 2022. This will be a one-year position. The salary is based on UCSB’s salary scales (roughly $45k/year, but depends on experience level).

Primary research duty is two-fold:

  1. the collection of behavioral data by interacting with blind individuals implanted with the Argus II Retinal Prosthesis System at our participating sites (Johns Hopkins University and the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor),

  2. the facilitation of visual psychophysics study at UC Santa Barbara.

More info and application instructions: https://recruit.ap.ucsb.edu/JPF02194.
