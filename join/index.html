Join Us

In general, we are looking for enthusiastic, computationally minded individuals with a shared passion for bionic vision. If you are interested in joining us, make sure we have open positions (see below) and check out our Lab Manual to familiarize yourself with our lab culture, policies, and expectations.

All Research Positions

We are completely full at the moment and for the foreseeable future. This includes any and all requests for research assistantships and internships—be it at the post-graduate, MS, undergraduate, or high-school level. Your request may therefore go unanswered.

We do sometimes look for RA help on specific projects (or with specific skillsets). In those cases, we will advertise the position via official university channels (e.g., departmental mailing lists), so keep an eye out for that.
